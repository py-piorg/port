FREQUENTLY ASKED

Common Questions

How long does a website take? +

Basic sites typically take 5-7 days. Standard packages take 1-2 weeks. Premium or complex projects depend on scope — we'll set a clear timeline before we start. I don't disappear mid-project.

What do you need from me? +

Your business name, what you do, any logo or branding you already have, colors you like, and examples of sites you admire. The more you give me, the better the result — but I can work with minimal info too.

Will my site work on phones? +

Yes — every site I build is fully responsive and mobile-first. Over 60% of web traffic comes from phones, so this is non-negotiable on every project.

How much does hosting cost? +

For most small business sites, I use Netlify's free tier — which means hosting costs you nothing. If you need a custom domain (like yourbusiness.com), that's typically $10-15/year from Namecheap or Google Domains.

Can I update the site myself after it's done? +

Yes. I can build it so you can edit text and images without touching code, or I can handle updates for you. We'll talk through what works best for your situation.

What if I don't like the result? +

Every package includes revision rounds. I don't consider a project done until you're happy with it. I'll work with you until we get it right.
